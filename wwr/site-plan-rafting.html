Overview

Purpose

This site will be to provide information on our white water tour services. Further, it will also let customers book tours with us. We will also be sharing testimonials from others that have previously used our services.

Audience

Who is the target audience? The target audience for this service will be anyone who has a desire to go on white water adventures. This could include scouts, school groups, families, and college students. The packages we will offer will be in a wide range of price points depending on the length of the trip and the extras a group decides to purchase for their trip. What do they want? They want to know what we offer, the price we offer it at, and the opinions of those that have previously used our services. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? All of our services will be on the site along with our additional services. People will be able to book our services on the site.
